a musical journey

Ever since I was five years old, I have been playing the violin. For a long time, it was a hobby I mostly practiced on my own, but for several years now I've been playing in orchestras as well.

Playing by myself

Since I started playing the violin almost fifteen years ago, I made a lot of progress. From playing only open strings and having trouble to hold my bow I now play concertos and pieces I could only dream of back then. Yet I still have a lot of progress to make.

Orchestras

Halfway through highschool, I had some trouble keeping up the discipline needed to practice every day and find motivation. Then, in 2016, I joined the Amersfoorts Jeugd Orkest, which helped me give a new sense of enjoyment to my instrument as it became a social activity as well. I continued to play for the AJO for five years and currently play in the Utrechtsch Studenten Orkest and (during my exchange in Paris) in the philharmonic orchestra of COGE (Chœurs et Orchestres des Grandes Écoles).
